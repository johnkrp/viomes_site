## Executive Summary

**VIOMES S.A.** — _Since 1982, designing and manufacturing in Greece._

VIOMES is an established manufacturer and distributor of **high-quality plastic products for home and garden** that combine quality, functionality, and modern aesthetics. With over 40 years of heritage, deep expertise in materials science, continuous equipment evolution, and meticulous attention to detail, VIOMES has built:

- **Strong position in the Greek market** (domestic leader in plastic home & garden products)
- **Significant international footprint** (exports across Europe and Mediterranean region)
- **Brand promise**: Reliability, durability, timeless design

**Core Differentiators** (from official positioning):

1. **Heritage & Trust** (1982 → 2026): 40+ years of proven reliability and expertise
2. **Made in Greece**: Greek manufacturing excellence, precision engineering, quality control
3. **Sustainability as Philosophy**: Integrated across all operations, not just marketing messaging
4. **Product Quality**: Responsibly engineered, built to last generations
5. **Customer-Centric**: Relationships built on trust, respect for people and environment

This MRD captures market dynamics, competitive landscape, and user trends to inform website strategy that authentically communicates VIOMES' heritage, quality, and values to B2B distributors, business customers, and end consumers.

## Customer Research & Personas

### Persona 1: B2B Distributor / Wholesaler (PRIMARY - Revenue Driver)

- **Age & Background**: 40-60, business decision-maker
- **Role**: Purchasing manager, sales director, warehouse manager, facilities director
- **Organization**: Regional wholesaler, garden center chain, landscaping company, DIY retailer
- **Goals**:
  - Find reliable supplier for bulk orders
  - Compare pricing and volume-based discounts
  - Verify supply capacity and reliability
  - Assess company stability and heritage (trust signal)
  - Establish long-term partnership
- **Pain Points**:
  - Needs detailed catalog with multiple sourcing options
  - Wants reliable bulk pricing and payment terms (30/60 days)
  - Requires predictable availability and quick response times
  - Values established relationships over commodity resellers
- **Device Preference**: Desktop (80%, work hours), mobile (20%, urgent checks)
- **Content Needs**:
  - Product specifications (dimensions, materials, color options)
  - Bulk pricing tiers and volume breaks
  - Company history and capacity information
  - Sustainability credentials and certifications
  - Case studies from similar businesses
  - Clear ordering and contact process
- **Decision Criteria**: Price competitiveness, product quality/durability, supply reliability, payment terms, delivery speed, company stability
- **Process**: 1-4 weeks research → phone/email inquiry to verify terms → negotiate → establish annual contract or recurring orders
- **Why VIOMES Wins**: 40-year heritage = stability guarantee; Greek manufacturing = quality/reliability reputation; direct relationship > commodity

### Persona 2: B2C End-Consumer (SECONDARY - Brand Building & DTC)

- **Age & Background**: 25-65, homeowner or gardening enthusiast
- **Roles**: Homeowner, urban gardener, property manager, professional landscaper
- **Goals**:
  - Find beautiful, durable planters/garden products
  - Express personal style through quality products
  - Support sustainable practices
  - Appreciate authentic heritage brand story
- **Pain Points**:
  - Hard to find quality products beyond mass-market retailers
  - Unclear product longevity and materials
  - Prefer brands with authentic story (not greenwashing)
  - Limited shipping/delivery information
- **Device Preference**: Mobile (70%, browsing + inspiration), Desktop (30%, research + comparison)
- **Content Needs**:
  - Beautiful product photography (multiple angles, styled)
  - Clear descriptions, materials, durability claims
  - Size guides and specification details
  - Customer reviews and testimonials
  - Company heritage and sustainability story
  - Shipping/delivery information and costs
  - Inspiration content (styling ideas, how-to)
- **Decision Criteria**: Aesthetics, durability, size/fit, value for money, sustainability commitment, brand authenticity
- **Process**: Inspiration (Pinterest/Instagram) → Google search → Website explore → Read reviews → Purchase (same-session or within 24h)
- **Why VIOMES Wins**: Premium alternative to mass-market plastic; authentic 40-year heritage; real sustainability commitment

### Persona 3: Search Engines / Crawlers (SYSTEM - SEO Infrastructure)

- **Goal**: Crawl, index, and rank site content for relevant keywords
- **Evaluation Signals**: Performance (Core Web Vitals), accessibility (WCAG AA), semantic structure, content quality, mobile-first indexing
- **Device Simulation**: Mobile crawler (mobile-first indexing standard)
- **Content Preferences**:
  - Structured data (JSON-LD for products, organization, local business)
  - Fast pages (<2.5s LCP, <3.8s FID)
  - Semantic HTML structure
  - Descriptive meta tags, H1/H2 hierarchy
  - Image alt text and descriptions
  - Internal linking strategy
- **Keywords to Rank**:
  - B2B: "wholesale planters", "garden products distributor", "planters bulk supplier", "VIOMES wholesale"
  - B2C: "quality planters", "sustainable garden products", "durable plant containers", "modern planters"
  - Brand: "VIOMES", "VIOMES products", "Greek planters", "made in Greece"

## User Journeys

### Journey 1: B2B Wholesaler / Distributor Research (PRIMARY REVENUE DRIVER)

1. **Discovery**: Google search ("wholesale planters suppliers", "garden products distributor", or referral from industry contact)
2. **Homepage evaluation**: Quick scan for company credibility, heritage, capacity, contact info
3. **Product catalog browse**: Review product range, specifications, styles (mobile sometimes, mostly desktop)
4. **Pricing & terms research**: Look for bulk pricing, payment terms, minimum orders, delivery information
5. **Company validation**: Read "About" page to assess stability and values (40-year heritage = trust signal)
6. **Contact & negotiate**: Email or phone inquiry to discuss terms, volume discounts, ordering process
7. **Ongoing relationship**: Annual contracts or recurring orders through established process

**KPIs**: Contact/inquiry form submissions, time-on-site (research depth), product page views, bounce rate (indicates professionalism)

### Journey 2: B2C Consumer Discovery (BRAND BUILDING)

1. **Inspiration**: Pinterest/Instagram or Google Search ("quality planters", "sustainable garden", "VIOMES products")
2. **Homepage exploration**: Brand story, product showcase, sustainability commitment
3. **Product browsing**: Filter by category, size, style; view detailed images and specifications
4. **Decision phase**: Read reviews (testimonials), check materials/durability, confirm shipping/costs
5. **Purchase**: Add to cart, checkout (Phase 2 - MVP may not include e-commerce yet)
6. **Post-purchase**: Brand advocacy, social sharing, potential repeat purchase

**KPIs**: Product page views, time-on-site, click paths, social shares, newsletter signups

### Journey 3: Brand Research & Heritage Interest (SECONDARY)

1. **Trigger**: Interested in sustainable companies, Greek manufacturing, heritage brands
2. **Navigation**: Explore "About" page, "Sustainability" section, company story
3. **Engagement**: Read company values, environmental commitments, product philosophy
4. **Outcome**: Decide to engage — contact for B2B partnership, make B2C purchase, share brand story

**KPIs**: Page depth, content time, engagement, conversion to contact form or email signup

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase Approach

**Phase 1 (MVP - Weeks 1-2): Digital Storefront Foundation**

- Showcase complete product catalog (structured, searchable, available for all viewing)
- Communicate heritage, quality, and sustainability positioning
- Enable B2B lead generation (contact forms, inquiry process)
- Optimize for SEO (B2B + brand searches)
- **Target**: Wholesalers/distributors researching online; B2C brand awareness

**Phase 2 (Q2 2026): E-commerce & Order Enablement**

- Add shopping cart, user accounts, checkout
- Enable direct consumer purchases (B2C revenue stream)
- Integrate with order management system (B2B + B2C)
- Add customer reviews and testimonials
- **Target**: DTC consumer sales; streamlined B2B ordering

**Phase 3+ (Post-Q2): Scaling & Personalization**

- Customer portal for B2B (order history, invoices, recurring orders)
- Personalization (recommended products, saved preferences)
- Wholesale program management (tiered pricing, bulk ordering)
- Customer data platform for targeted marketing
- **Target**: Customer retention, lifetime value growth

### Marketing Channels (Priority Order by Audience)

#### B2B Wholesaler / Distributor (PRIMARY)

1. **LinkedIn B2B Outreach** - Direct message to purchasing managers, sales directors - Target: "garden products wholesale", "distributor sourcing"
2. **Industry Trade Shows** - Physical + online presence - Distribute website URL + catalog
3. **Email Marketing** - Build list from industry contacts, send catalog + value proposition
4. **Google Search (B2B Keywords)** - Target: "wholesale planters", "bulk garden products", "VIOMES distributors"
5. **Sales Team Direct Outreach** - Equip sales with modern website as credibility asset

#### B2C Consumer (SECONDARY)

1. **SEO (Organic Search)** - High-ROI, long-term - Target: "quality planters", "sustainable garden", "VIOMES products"
2. **Social Media (Instagram, Pinterest)** - Visual inspiration, lifestyle content - Link to product collections
3. **Email Newsletter** - Build list on website, nurture with styling tips + new products
4. **Paid Search (Google Ads)** - Budget permitting, target high-intent keywords
5. **PR & Content Marketing** - "VIOMES heritage", "made in Greece" positioning

#### SEO Foundation (BOTH)

- Optimize for all keywords listed in Persona 3
- Core Web Vitals >85 (competitive SEO advantage)
- Semantic structure + JSON-LD (rich snippets)
- Mobile-first responsive design
